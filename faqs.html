What Do You Want To Know?

How Long can I Invest?.

You can invest for 1 year for a 36% annual return or monthly for 3% monthly return but with a minimum of two months investment period.

What do you Invest in?

Arich Invest is a product of Arich Loans Limited which offers business loans to retailers and traders. With your investment you Co-fund these business owners with us.

Is my money safe?.

We offer full guarantee on Investment because all our loans are insured against death, critical Illness, Burglary, Fire and Loss of Job for employees.

How do I Invest?

Click on the Fund Wallet Button and use Paystack Link to fund your investment. Or you make a transfer to an Arich Loans Limited account.

How can I withdraw my money?

Our withdrawal methods are Simple.
1. Your funds are transferred to your account at the end of your investment period automatically.
2. You can request to withdraw before the end of the period with a 24 hours notice to us.

Can I rollover my Investment?

Yes, you can rollover your investment by clicking on the request button and rollover button to make a rollover request.

Do you have a Physical Office?

Yes, we do. We have unit offices close to market clusters and you can walk into our office at 70 Obafemi Awolowo Way Ikeja, Lagos. It is best to make an appointment.
